Privacy Policy

Last Updated: October 27, 2025

Welcome to OmniNutri ("we," "our," or "us"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the security of your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your data when you use our mobile application.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Information You Provide

  • Personal Profile: Name, age, gender, height, weight, activity level, and health goals
  • Food Log Data: Foods you track, portions, meal times, and nutritional intake records
  • User Preferences: Language settings, dietary preferences, and app customization

1.2 Automatically Collected Information

  • Device Information: Device type, operating system version, and app version
  • Usage Data: Features used, time spent in app, and interaction patterns (anonymized)
  • Crash Reports: Technical data to improve app stability (no personal information included)

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Provide comprehensive nutrition analysis and personalized recommendations
  • Calculate and track your daily nutritional intake
  • Generate weekly and monthly nutrition reports
  • Identify nutritional gaps and provide supplement suggestions
  • Improve app functionality and user experience
  • Respond to your support requests and feedback

3. Data Storage and Security

3.1 Local Storage

All your nutrition data is stored locally on your device using secure, encrypted storage. We do not upload your food logs or personal health data to any external servers.

3.2 Security Measures

  • Industry-standard encryption for data at rest
  • Secure local database with access controls
  • Regular security updates and patches
  • No third-party data sharing without explicit consent

4. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties, except in the following limited circumstances:

  • With Your Consent: When you explicitly authorize us to share specific information
  • Legal Requirements: When required by law, court order, or government regulation
  • Service Providers: Analytics services (anonymized data only) to improve app performance

5. In-App Purchases

OmniNutri offers premium features through in-app purchases:

  • Monthly Subscription ($2.99/month)
  • Annual Subscription ($19.99/year)
  • Lifetime Access ($49.99 one-time)

All payment processing is handled securely by Apple through the App Store. We do not collect or store your payment information.

6. Your Rights and Choices

You have the right to:

  • Access Your Data: View all data stored in the app at any time
  • Delete Your Data: Clear all your nutrition logs and personal information from the app
  • Export Your Data: Download your nutrition records in a portable format
  • Opt-Out of Analytics: Disable anonymous usage tracking in app settings

7. Children's Privacy

OmniNutri is not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe we have inadvertently collected such information, please contact us immediately.

8. International Users

OmniNutri is available globally. Since all data is stored locally on your device, your information remains in your jurisdiction and is subject to your local data protection laws.

9. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any significant changes through:

  • In-app notifications
  • Updated "Last Updated" date on this page
  • Email notification (if you have provided your email)

10. Third-Party Services

OmniNutri may use the following third-party services:

  • Apple App Store: For app distribution and in-app purchases
  • Anonymous Analytics: To understand app usage patterns (no personal data collected)

11. California Privacy Rights (CCPA)

If you are a California resident, you have additional rights under the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA):

  • Right to know what personal information is collected
  • Right to delete personal information
  • Right to opt-out of the sale of personal information (we do not sell your data)
  • Right to non-discrimination for exercising your privacy rights

12. GDPR Compliance (European Users)

For users in the European Economic Area (EEA), we comply with GDPR requirements:

  • Legal basis for processing: Your consent and legitimate interests
  • Data retention: Until you delete the app or clear your data
  • Right to access, rectification, erasure, and portability
  • Right to lodge a complaint with a supervisory authority

13. Contact Us

If you have any questions, concerns, or requests regarding this Privacy Policy or your personal data, please contact us:

  • Email: omniapps@proton.me
  • App: Use the "Contact Support" feature in the app settings

We will respond to your inquiry within 30 days.
